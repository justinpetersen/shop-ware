About Shop-Ware

Made by people who know auto repair

 

Our belief...

Auto repair is a challenging and rewarding business, accomplished by hard working, intelligent professionals.

 
 

What We Do

We tailor innovative, web-based technology to repair shops. We provide valuable, reliable tools, based on real world experience, flexible to meet your independent needs.

We believe in the marketplace and helping shops compete. Customers benefit from documentation to determine the value of services rendered; Shop-Ware develops and supports products to showcase your work, maximize convenience, and demonstrate value.

Our Background

Shop-Ware owes its roots to Luscious Garage, a hybrid specialty shop that created its own shop management system in 2007. Catered to tech-savvy hybrid owners, "Hyspace" offered transparent access to work orders and invoices in a live, paperless, web-based format. The software proved a cornerstone of LG's success, garnering national attention. It continued to evolve over the years to better suit the needs of Luscious Garage and other shops that expressed interest in using it.

Our Founder

Carolyn Coquillette owns and operates Luscious Garage in San Francisco. LG continues to cultivate market share, with technical expertise, online content, and efficiency at the forefront of its strategy. Shop software remains key to its competitive advantage.

Carolyn cut her teeth in Ann Arbor, MI, receiving a Master ASE Certification with L1 in 2002. Other wall hangings include a Bachelors in Physics and English (University of Michigan, 2000), which hopefully informs the quality of her blog posts. She's a proud and active sponsoring member of iATN.
